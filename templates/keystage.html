Key stage results 1 and 2 are stages just for primary school students. The graphs present the percentage of students that have passed in four distinct subjects for stage 1 and three subjects for stage 2. These are Reading, Writing, Mathematics and Science (KS1). The options bar is used to switch between the two stages and the buttons for years. The boroughs are split into two halves, sorted by alphabetical order. The button will change the figure and present the information for the new boroughs accordingly.
