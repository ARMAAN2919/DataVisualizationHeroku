Education in London is a Web Application illustrating interactive visuals. It is open to the public to visualize different sectors of education, from the past and present. The main objective is for the authorities to form an analysis from the graphs to determine the lack of investments and solutions for the educational system in London. The application uses the data available on London Datastore, as the Greater London Authority has worked closely to develop the system.

The application is straightforward and easy to navigate. The header consists of seven unique graphs to visualize from, topics ranging from results to higher education and beyond. As mentioned, all figures have interactive functionalities, which provide additional information in a simple but aesthetic process. The interactions consist of options, sliders and buttons; it is clear what they are able to do and are simple to execute. All graphs can only be visualized individually. If comparisons between two graphs or more are needed, a new window/tab running the application is required. Please do have a look around, and gain insights on different areas of education.
